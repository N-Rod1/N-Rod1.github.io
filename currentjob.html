Property Tax Appraiser

As a property tax appraiser my job is to value Real Property for property tax purposes. I investigate and analyze data in the appraisal of all types of real property. I perform field studies and appraisals of residential, commercial and agricultural properties. I also analyze and estimate construction costs and depreciation for residential and rural properties. As a lead appraiser I gather information, measure and plot land and improvements, while reading and interpreting blueprints. I train new appraisers and assist in them obtaining their Appraiser Certification through the California Board of Equalization.
