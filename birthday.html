My Birthday!

October 7th of 1992!

There's nothing exciting about the date but I really love October because it's when it finally starts looking like fall outside. Fall is my favorite season, because of the natural colour of the leaves changing, the weather cooling down and all festivity that surrounds it. I enjoy the holidays that are in Fall mainly Halloween and of course pumpkin spice everything. So, having my birthday coincide with my favorite season is the best and gives me something to look forward to.
